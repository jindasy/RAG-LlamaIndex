Admission information for the Software and Knowledge Engineering Degree Program of Kasetsart University of 2025 (TCAS 2567).

International Curriculum and English Curriculum Project (TCAS Round 1)
Number of applicants: 30 people
Specific qualifications of applicants
1. Currently studying or graduating from Mathayom 6, or currently studying in the final year, or graduating from high school from abroad. or have a GED or equivalent qualification. Applicants who have taken the GED test from May 2017 onwards must take the GED test in 4 subjects, with each subject having to score at least 145 points.
2. Cumulative grade point average (GPAX) for 5 semesters not less than 2.50 or equivalent.
3. One of the following test scores:
  3.1 SAT Mathematics test score of not less than 600 points and Evidence-Based Reading & Writing combined with Mathematics of not less than 1,000 points and English subject standardized test scores. Which one?
  - TOEFL (IBT) test score not less than 61 points or equivalent.
  - IELTS test score of not less than 5.5 points or equivalent.
  - Duolingo test score not less than 95 points or equivalent.
  3.2 Cumulative grade point average for English, Physics, and Mathematics subjects. At the 4th and 5th year of high school, each subject must not be less than 2.50 out of a full score of 4.00 or equivalent.
  3.3 For applicants who are studying in their final year. or have graduated from high school from abroad or have a GED or equivalent qualification, submit documents showing that they are currently studying in their final year or have graduated.
4. Work history (Portfolio)
Selection criteria
1. Work history (Portfolio) (should have work that matches the field you want to apply for, such as software field. Should have work in programming, etc.)
2. English language scores or level of ability to use English
3. The interview will be in English, considering
  3.1 Academic questions/English usage
  3.2 Attitude and suitability for education

Admission Project (TCAS Round 3)
Number of applicants: 10 people
Minimum conditions
1. TGAT General Aptitude not less than 1.00 points
2. TPAT3 Aptitude for science, technology, engineering, not less than 1.00 points
3. A-Level Math1 Applied Mathematics 1 not less than 1.00 points
4. A-Level Phy Physics not less than 1.00 points
Consideration criteria
1. TGAT general aptitude proportion 20.00
2. TPAT3 Aptitude for Science, Technology, Engineering, proportion 50.00
3. A-Level Math1 Applied Mathematics 1 proportion 25.00
4. A-Level Phy Physics proportion 5.00
